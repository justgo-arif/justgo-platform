# FR-087

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.9. Asset Registers
Source heading: 3.6.9. Asset Registers

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to create, submit, or process asset registers records while preserving required business data so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
