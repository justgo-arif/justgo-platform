# FR-069

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.5. Asset Leases
Source heading: 3.6.5. Asset Leases

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to create, submit, or process asset leases records while preserving required business data so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
