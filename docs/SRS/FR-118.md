# FR-118

Feature: 3.9. Finance and Payment Management
Subsection: 3.9.8. Subscriptions
Source heading: 3.9.8. Subscriptions

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to download or export subscriptions information in a usable form so that organisations can manage payments, balances, recurring plans, products, and payment accounts.
