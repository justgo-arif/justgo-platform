# FR-027

Feature: 3.2. Member Profile Management
Subsection: 3.2.3. Member Family
Source heading: 3.2.3. Member Family

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to search, filter, page, and review member family records so that members and support users can maintain accurate personal, family, contact, and preference information.
