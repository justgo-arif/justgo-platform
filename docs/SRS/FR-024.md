# FR-024

Feature: 3.2. Member Profile Management
Subsection: 3.2.3. Member Family
Source heading: 3.2.3. Member Family

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to create, submit, or process member family records while preserving required business data so that members and support users can maintain accurate personal, family, contact, and preference information.
