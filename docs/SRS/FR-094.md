# FR-094

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.10. Asset Reports
Source heading: 3.6.10. Asset Reports

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to download or export asset reports information in a usable form so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
