# FR-071

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.5. Asset Leases
Source heading: 3.6.5. Asset Leases

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to review asset leases history, activity, and audit information so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
