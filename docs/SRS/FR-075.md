# FR-075

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.6. Asset Licenses
Source heading: 3.6.6. Asset Licenses

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to create, submit, or process asset licenses records while preserving required business data so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
