# FR-064

Feature: 3.6. Asset Management
Subsection: 3.6.4. Asset Credentials
Source heading: 3.6.4. Asset Credentials

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized users to create, submit, or process asset credentials records while preserving required business data so that asset owners and administrators can maintain controlled, auditable asset operations.
