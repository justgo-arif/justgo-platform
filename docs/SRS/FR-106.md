# FR-106

Feature: 3.9. Finance and Payment Management
Subsection: 3.9.1. Balances
Source heading: 3.9.1. Balances

## Requirement

The system SHALL allow authorized finance users to review and manage products, balances, plans, subscriptions, installments, and billing history so that organisations can manage payments, balances, recurring plans, products, and payment accounts.

## Endpoint Trace IDs

EP-271, EP-272
